Hiring Process
What to Expect: Our Engineering Interview Process
A clear, structured journey from first conversation to offer — built to be transparent and respect your time.
1
30 min · Recruiter (Nick or Lina)
HR Interview
Cultural fit, salary expectations, availability, and a basic qualification check.
2
60 min · Hiring Manager
Discovery Call
General fit (technical & cultural), verifying the role match, plus a check on salary expectations and start date.
3
60 min · 2 Engineers (1 Staff/Principal for Staff+ roles, or 2 Seniors for Senior and below)
Technical Discovery Call
A deep technical discussion — past projects, system design, and engineering capabilities.
4
~4 hours · Independent work
Take-Home Assignment
You work independently and AI tools are encouraged. The solution is submitted via GitHub, and 1–2 reviewers assess quality before proceeding.
5
90 min · 2 Engineers (Staff/Principal + Senior)
Case Study Interview
We discuss your take-home solution together. You'll apply changes without AI tools to demonstrate genuine code ownership.
6
Owned by Hiring Manager
Reference Check
Minimum 1–2 professional references. You'll be asked for them during the Final Interview, and the Hiring Manager conducts the reference call.
7
min. 45 min · Ben Blackmore (CTO) or Judith Redi (VP of Engineering)
Final Interview
Product vision, knowledge of the observability space, leadership (for staff-level roles), and communication.
